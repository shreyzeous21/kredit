About Us

KreditVenture is a pioneering NBFC which strongly believes that Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) are the backbone of India's economy, driving innovation, employment, and economic growth. They account for a significant portion of the country's GDP and provide livelihoods for millions across diverse sectors.


Our innovative approach focuses on expanding financial inclusion by reaching out to the remote and underserved areas that traditional banks often overlook. We have embarked on our journey into secured small business loans (Micro LAP), aiming to address a market of ₹20 lakh crore, which currently has a penetration rate of less than 10%.

WHAT WE OFFER

Secured Business Loans

Our secured business loans provide borrowers with the necessary capital for refinancing existing debt, purchasing property or equipment, or improving business operations. Our flexible repayment options enable borrowers to efficiently manage financial risks and adversities.
